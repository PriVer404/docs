## How To Review A Contract

<Warning>
  You must have a "fresh" .docx document with no redlined changes.
</Warning>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open The Contract In Word" titleSize="h3">
    Open the Word file for the contract
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open The ContractKen Word Add-In" titleSize="h3">
    <Info>
      If you're not currently logged in, the add-in will prompt you to _login/signup_
    </Info>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Review Contract" titleSize="h3">
    From the 3 options on the screen, select **Review Contract**.

    <Note>
      If your word document contains redlines, you need to either accept or reject the changes.
    </Note>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review Setup" titleSize="h3">
    <img
      src="/images/image.png"
      alt="image.png"
      title="image.png"
      style={{ width:"52%" }}
    />

    1. From the **Review For** dropdown, _select the name of the party_ that you're representing and want to review the contract from the perspective of.
    2. Click on **Begin Review**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Done" titleSize="h3">
    Once you've clicked on **Begin Review**, our AI service gets to work, analyzing and reviewing the contract in detail. While our AI service automtically starts working on the Insights and Summaries, you can explore the host of on-demand features we have available as a part of the Review Mode.
  </Step>
</Steps>
